New to Fontana CA?

These are the top 3 locations I've been to.

Have you been here?

Fontana Park

This may be the best park in the city. Besides the playground and large field of grass, it contains an aquatic park and a skatepark. A very nice place for a picnic.

Bell Air Swap Meet

Remember to bring your monies to aquire some interesting items and perhaps treat yourself to some food. Pay for admission!

Phu Chen Kitchen

This is where to get delicious chineese food! Food is made fresh so expect to wait some time for your order but the service is excellent.
